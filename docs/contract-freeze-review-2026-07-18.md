## 3. codemod 写像表 v1 の要点

- **確定写像（議事録そのまま）**: fg→text-primary／fg-muted→text-muted／fg-faint→text-faint／
  fg-inverse→text-inverse／ok→success／warning→warn／accent-contrast→on-accent／*-ink→on-*／
  primary・muted(origin)→text-primary・text-muted／line(vault)→border／
  accent-weak→accent-soft・brand-violet→x-brand-violet・danger-hover→x-danger-hover（AM-3）

  danger-weak→danger-soft（records 現物の -weak 群）／records sidebar-* → x-sidebar-*

  surface-2→surface-overlay・sunk→surface-sunken・navy→accent（hover/soft/on 同型）・
  warning-ink→on-warn・brass/seal/rail/ink 系→x- 送り
